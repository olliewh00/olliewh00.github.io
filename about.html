About Me

Hello! I'm Ryan Oliver, a fresh graduate with a focus on creating robust and scalable applications. I enjoy solving complex problems and continuously learning new technologies.

My journey in tech started during my time as a student in Politehnika Wroclawska, where my course of Electronics and Computer Science Engineering took me to understand the world of electronics, microcontrollers and Programming. During my time as a student I got to learn about AI, ML and Computer Vision which resonated with me and made me create projects on these topics.

Outside of coding. I volunteer at ESN (Erasmus Student Network). An organisation that helps international students settle in a new country. I was the local Project Manager at my university as well as the Audit Commission member. Following my tenure in the local level, I took my passion for technology and support to join the IT team as the Support Specialist for ESN Poland. A role that i am currently volunteering for. I also enjoy playing football, fortnite and going to the gym.
